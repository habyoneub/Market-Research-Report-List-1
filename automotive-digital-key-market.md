<p><strong>The Automotive Digital Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RKES</li><li>PKES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Digital Key market can be divided into two primary types: RKES (Remote Keyless Entry System) and PKES (Passive Keyless Entry System). RKES systems allow users to unlock and start their vehicles remotely using a smartphone or a key fob, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, PKES systems enable keyless access and ignition by detecting the presence of an authenticated device, eliminating the need for physical keys altogether. Both systems enhance user experience while improving vehicle security.</p></p>
