<p><strong>The Gooseneck Trailer Hitches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under-bed Gooseneck</li><li>Over/Above-bed Gooseneck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gooseneck trailer hitch market is primarily categorized into two types: under-bed gooseneck and over/above-bed gooseneck hitches. Under-bed gooseneck hitches are mounted beneath the truck bed, providing a clean appearance and allowing full use of the truck bed when not in use. In contrast, over/above-bed gooseneck hitches are installed on top of the truck bed, making them easily accessible and visible. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of towing capability, ease of installation, and convenience for users.</p></p>
