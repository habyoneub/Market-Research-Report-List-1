<p><p>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) is an advanced catalytic process used to reduce nitrogen oxides (NOx) emissions in exhaust gases from diesel engines. This technology is increasingly implemented in various industries, particularly in automotive and industrial applications, to meet stringent emission regulations and enhance air quality.</p><p>The SCR market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, growing demand for cleaner transportation solutions, and the expansion of the automotive sector.
