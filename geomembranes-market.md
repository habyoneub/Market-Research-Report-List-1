<p><strong>The Geomembranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous Geomembranes</li><li>Thermoplastic Geomembranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geomembranes market is primarily categorized into two types: bituminous and thermoplastic geomembranes. Bituminous geomembranes are made from asphalt materials, providing excellent waterproofing and chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications like landfill liners and containment facilities. In contrast, thermoplastic geomembranes, which include materials like PVC and HDPE, are known for their flexibility, durability, and ease of installation. They are commonly used in mining, agriculture, and water management applications due to their versatile properties and environmental adaptability.</p></p>
