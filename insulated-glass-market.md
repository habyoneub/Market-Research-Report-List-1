<p><p>The insulated glass market features several specialized types. Low-emission (Low-E) glass minimizes infrared and ultraviolet light without compromising visible light, enhancing energy efficiency. Fully tempered glass offers increased strength and safety due to its heat treatment. Soft coat Low-E glass has a reflective coating applied during manufacturing, optimizing thermal performance, while hard coat Low-E glass is treated post-production for durability. Clear annealed glass and clear laminated glass, both from 1/8” to 1/4” thickness, provide insulation and sound reduction. Bulletproof glass offers enhanced security for high-risk environments, combining multiple layers for impact resistance.</p></p>
