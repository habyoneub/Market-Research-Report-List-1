<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) equipment facilitates the simultaneous transmission of multiple signals over a single optical fiber by using various wavelengths. The market is primarily divided into two types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) equipment, which utilizes wider spacing between wavelengths, allowing for fewer channels but at lower cost, making it suitable for shorter distances and lower data rates; and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM) equipment, which employs closely spaced wavelengths, offering higher capacity and longer reach, ideal for high-capacity networks.</p></p>
