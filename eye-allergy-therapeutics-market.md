<p><p>The eye allergy therapeutics market comprises three main types: antihistamines, corticosteroids, and immunomodulators. Antihistamines block histamine receptors, alleviating symptoms such as itching and redness. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and control immune responses, providing relief for severe cases. Immunomodulators, including agents that modify immune system activity, help manage chronic allergic conditions by reducing sensitivity to allergens. Together, these therapies address various allergic reactions affecting the eyes, offering patients multiple options for effective symptom management and improved quality of life.</p></p>
