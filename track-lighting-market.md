<p><p>The track lighting market is primarily categorized into three types: Halogen, LED, and Incandescent track lighting. Halogen track lighting offers bright, warm illumination and is often valued for its color rendering but has shorter lifespan and higher energy consumption. LED track lighting is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and available in various color temperatures, making it increasingly popular. Incandescent track lighting provides a soft, warm glow but uses more energy and has a shorter lifespan, positioning it as a more traditional option.</p></p>
