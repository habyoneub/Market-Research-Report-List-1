<p><p>A Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Transformer (VPIT) is a type of electrical transformer that utilizes a vacuum pressure impregnation process to enhance the insulation of its components. This method involves placing the transformer in a vacuum chamber and impregnating it with a resin, which effectively fills any voids and spaces in the insulation materials. This results in improved dielectric strength, reduced moisture absorption, and enhanced durability, making VPITs particularly suitable for harsh operating environments.</p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Transformer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.30% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for efficient energy solutions, the rising need for reliable power supply in industrial applications, and ongoing technological advancements in transformer design. Emerging economies are also witnessing a surge in infrastructure development, propelling the need for advanced transformers.
