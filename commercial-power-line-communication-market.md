<p><p>The Commercial Power Line Communication (PLC) market is divided into two main types: narrowband and broadband. Narrowband PLC focuses on low data transmission rates, ideal for applications like smart metering and remote monitoring, where low bandwidth suffices. In contrast, broadband PLC offers higher data rates, facilitating internet access and multimedia services, suitable for high-speed connectivity in homes and businesses. Both types leverage existing power lines, enhancing communication without extensive infrastructure investment, catering to distinct operational needs in various sectors.</p></p>
