<p><p>Milling-turning centers are advanced machine tools combining milling and turning capabilities, categorized into vertical and horizontal markets. Vertical milling-turning centers feature a vertical spindle orientation, allowing for efficient machining of complex shapes and contours, ideal for small to medium-sized parts. In contrast, horizontal centers utilize a horizontal spindle, providing enhanced stability and chip removal, making them suitable for larger, heavier workpieces with high production volumes. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, optimizing precision machining processes.</p></p>
