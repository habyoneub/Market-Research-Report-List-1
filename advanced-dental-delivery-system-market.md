<p><strong>The Advanced Dental Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rear-Delivery System</li><li>Side-Delivery Delivery System</li><li>Over-the-Patient System</li><li>Over-the-Head Delivery System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Dental Delivery System market comprises various types designed for enhanced ergonomics and efficiency. The Rear-Delivery System positions equipment behind the operator, offering unobstructed access to the patient. The Side-Delivery System places instruments to the side, allowing for easy reach and visibility. The Over-the-Patient System features a suspended tray above the patient, maximizing workspace. Lastly, the Over-the-Head Delivery System positions tools and instruments directly overhead, facilitating quick access while maintaining a clear view of the treatment area.</p></p>
