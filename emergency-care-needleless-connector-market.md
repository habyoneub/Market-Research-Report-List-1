<p><strong>The Emergency Care Needleless Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Care Needleless Connector market features three main types: Positive Fluid Displacement, Negative Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. Positive Fluid Displacement connectors actively push fluids through the system to minimize blood reflux, reducing the risk of clots. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors allow residual blood to remain in the hub, which can potentially lead to occlusion. Neutral Displacement connectors maintain a balance, facilitating fluid flow without significant reflux or residual volume, enhancing patient safety and improving fluid management during emergency care.</p></p>
