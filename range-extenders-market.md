<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Range Extenders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Band (2.4 GHz) Range Extenders</li><li>Dual Band (2.4 GHz+5 GHz) Range Extenders</li><li>Tri-Band (2.4 GHz+5 GHz+5 GHz) Range Extenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The range extenders market consists of three main types based on frequency bands. Single Band (2.4 GHz) range extenders operate solely on the 2.4 GHz frequency, offering basic coverage. Dual Band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz) range extenders utilize both frequencies, enhancing performance and reducing congestion for better speed. Tri-Band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz + 5 GHz) range extenders provide the highest capacity by adding an additional 5 GHz band, optimizing data flow for multiple devices and improving overall network stability.</p></p>
