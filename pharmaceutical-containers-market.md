<p><p>The pharmaceutical containers market comprises three main types: glass, plastic, and metal containers. Glass containers, favored for their inertness and ability to preserve sterility, are commonly used for injectable drugs and vaccines. Plastic containers, lightweight and versatile, are popular for oral medications and convenience packaging. Metal containers, often used for aerosol products and certain pharmaceuticals, offer durability and protection from light and moisture. Each type serves distinct needs based on safety, compatibility, and material properties in pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
