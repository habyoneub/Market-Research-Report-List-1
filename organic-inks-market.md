<p><p>The organic inks market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Solvent-Based** inks use organic solvents as the base, offering vibrant colors and good adhesion but may emit volatile organic compounds.</p><p>2. **Water-Based** inks, more environmentally friendly, utilize water as the primary medium, providing reduced emissions and safe handling.</p><p>3. **Oil-Based** inks utilize natural oils, offering rich pigmentation and smooth application but may have longer drying times.</p><p>4. **Other** types encompass a variety of specialty inks tailored for specific applications, such as UV-curable or soy-based inks.</p></p>
