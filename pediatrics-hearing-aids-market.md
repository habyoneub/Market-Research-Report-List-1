<p><p>The pediatric hearing aids market encompasses various types catering to children's unique needs. Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids are worn behind the ear and are adjustable for growth. In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids fit directly within the outer ear, providing a custom fit. Hearing implants, such as cochlear implants, offer solutions for severe hearing loss by directly stimulating the auditory nerve. Other devices may include bone conduction aids and wearable technologies, enhancing overall hearing access and auditory development for children.</p></p>
