<p><p>Blockchain in healthcare and wellness enhances data security, interoperability, and patient privacy. Public blockchains offer transparency and decentralization, allowing general access but with potential scalability issues. Private blockchains restrict access to authorized entities, ensuring data control and regulatory compliance, ideal for organizations managing sensitive health records. Each type serves distinct purposes in improving healthcare data management and patient care.</p></p>
