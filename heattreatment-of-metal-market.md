<p><p>The heat treatment of metal market is categorized into two main types: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment involves processes like annealing, quenching, and tempering, aimed at altering the overall properties of the metal to enhance strength, ductility, and hardness. In contrast, surface heat treatment focuses on modifying the surface layer of the metal to improve wear resistance, fatigue strength, and corrosion resistance, often through techniques such as carburizing and nitriding. Both types serve essential industrial applications.</p></p>
