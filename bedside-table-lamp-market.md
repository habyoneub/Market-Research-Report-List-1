<p><p>The bedside table lamp market comprises three primary types: LED, halogen, and incandescent. LED lamps are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and available in various designs, making them popular for modern settings. Halogen lamps provide bright, white light and are known for their compact size but consume more energy than LEDs. Incandescent lamps offer a warm, inviting glow and a classic aesthetic, though they are less energy-efficient and have a shorter lifespan, contributing to their declining popularity in favor of more sustainable options.</p></p>
