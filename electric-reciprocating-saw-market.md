<p><p>The electric reciprocating saw market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired reciprocating saws are powered by electricity through a cord, providing constant power and typically offering higher cutting performance for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, wireless reciprocating saws operate on rechargeable batteries, granting users greater portability and convenience, ideal for jobs requiring mobility or access to tight spaces. Both types are popular in construction, woodworking, and demolition, catering to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
