<p><strong>The Furniture Varnish Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture varnish market is primarily divided into two types: waterborne and solventborne varnishes. Waterborne varnishes utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly and ideal for interior applications. Conversely, solventborne varnishes use organic solvents, providing a durable finish and better penetration into wood, but they typically have higher VOC levels. Both types cater to diverse preferences in terms of durability, application ease, drying time, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
