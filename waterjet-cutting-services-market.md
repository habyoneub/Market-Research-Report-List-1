<p><ul><li>Pure Water Jet Cutting</li><li>Abrasive Water Jet Cutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutting services are categorized primarily into two types: pure water jet cutting and abrasive water jet cutting. Pure water jet cutting utilizes a high-pressure stream of water to cut softer materials like rubber and foam, making it ideal for applications requiring precision without heat-affected zones. Abrasive water jet cutting, on the other hand, incorporates abrasive materials into the water stream, allowing it to effectively cut harder materials such as metal, stone, and glass with high precision and versatility.</p></p>
