<p><p>The digital amplifier market includes two main types: monoblock amplifiers and multi-channel amplifiers. Monoblock amplifiers are single-channel devices that deliver high power to a single audio source, ideal for subwoofers in home theater systems. In contrast, multi-channel amplifiers provide multiple outputs, allowing them to power several speakers simultaneously, making them suitable for surround sound setups. Both types enhance audio performance and are popular in various applications, including home audio systems, automotive sound systems, and professional audio setups. </p></p>
