<p><p>The mRNA raw enzyme market encompasses various enzymes essential for mRNA synthesis and processing. Restriction enzymes are used for DNA manipulation, while RNA polymerases synthesize RNA from a DNA template. Capping enzymes add a protective cap to mRNA, and inorganic pyrophosphatases remove unwanted phosphate groups. RNase inhibitors protect RNA from degradation, 2'-O-methyltransferases modify RNA for stability, and Poly(A) polymerases add polyadenylation tails. DNase I is utilized to eliminate unwanted DNA, alongside other specialized enzymes serving niche applications in RNA work.</p></p>
