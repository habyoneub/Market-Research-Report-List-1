<p><p>The In Vitro Diagnostic (IVD) antibody market is primarily categorized into three types: monoclonal antibodies (mAbs), polyclonal antibodies (pAbs), and recombinant antibodies (rAbs). mAbs are uniform antibodies targeting specific antigens, making them ideal for diagnostics and targeted therapies. pAbs are derived from multiple immune cells, providing a broad spectrum of recognition, which enhances sensitivity but lacks specificity. rAbs are engineered using recombinant DNA technology, offering consistency and scalability, essential for modern diagnostic applications.</p></p>
