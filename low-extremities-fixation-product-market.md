<p><p>The Low Extremities Fixation Product Market comprises various types of internal and external fixation devices used to stabilize fractures in the lower limbs. Internal fixation includes plates, screws, nails, and other devices that are surgically implanted to maintain bone alignment. Plates provide surface support, screws anchor fragments, and nails allow for intramedullary stabilization. External fixation involves devices applied outside the body, holding bones in correct position while healing. These products enhance recovery and improve functional outcomes for patients with lower limb injuries.</p></p>
