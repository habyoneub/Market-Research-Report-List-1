<p><p>The ankle stabilizer market includes various types, primarily lace-ups and hinged types. Lace-up ankle stabilizers offer adjustable support and compression, making them suitable for mild to moderate injuries and providing a customizable fit. Hinged ankle stabilizers, on the other hand, incorporate rigid or semi-rigid frames with mechanical hinges, providing enhanced stability and range of motion, typically used for more severe injuries or post-surgery rehabilitation. Both types cater to different patient needs, ensuring effective ankle support and protection.</p></p>
