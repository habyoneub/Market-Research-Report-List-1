<p><p>Cathode materials play a crucial role in battery performance, with various types suited for different applications. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LiCoO2) offers high energy density, ideal for smartphones. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LiMn2O4) delivers improved thermal stability and safety for electric vehicles. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4 or LFP) provides excellent cycle life and stability, favored in energy storage systems. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (LiNiMnCoO2 or NMC) balances energy density and thermal stability, making it popular in electric vehicles and industrial applications.</p></p>
