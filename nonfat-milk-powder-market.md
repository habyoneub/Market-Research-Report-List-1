<p><p>Nonfat Milk Powder (NFMP) is a dairy product created by evaporating the moisture from pasteurized skim milk, resulting in a powder with minimal fat content. In addition, the expanding applications in sectors like infant formula and sports nutrition contribute to the market's upward trajectory.
