<p><p>The automotive camshaft market comprises three main types: cast camshafts, forged steel camshafts, and assembled camshafts. Cast camshafts are manufactured using molten metal, providing cost-effectiveness and complexity in design. Forged steel camshafts are created through shaping solid steel, offering superior strength and durability, ideal for high-performance applications. Assembled camshafts consist of multiple components joined together, allowing for customization and flexibility in performance. Each type serves distinct needs in terms of performance, reliability, and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
