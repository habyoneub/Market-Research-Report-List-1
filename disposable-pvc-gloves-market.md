<p><p>The disposable PVC gloves market is segmented into powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a light dusting of powder, such as cornstarch, making them easier to don and doff. They offer enhanced comfort in prolonged use but may cause irritation for some users. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are free from any powder, minimizing the risk of allergic reactions and contamination, making them suitable for sensitive environments like healthcare settings. Both types cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
