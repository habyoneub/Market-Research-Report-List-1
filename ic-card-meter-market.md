<p><strong>The IC Card Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Contactless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC card meter market is divided into two main types: contact type and contactless type. Contact type meters require physical contact between the card and the meter for data exchange, ensuring secure transactions but limiting convenience. In contrast, contactless type meters utilize radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to perform transactions without direct contact, enhancing convenience and speed. Both types serve various applications, including electricity, water, and gas metering, catering to different user preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>
